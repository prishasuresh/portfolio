ABOUT ME ✦

Hello, I'm Prisha!


I'm currently a senior at UC Berkeley majoring in Media Studies and Economics. Over the last few years, I've held a number of different marketing roles, both technical and creative, and I've always loved the process of deep-diving into a brand, engaging with their audiences, and crafting the perfect story.


Professionally, I currently lead UC Berkeley's chapter of the American Advertising Federation, a student-led agency with over 40 active members that participates in contract work with a variety of brands and competes in the National Student Advertising Competition against 200+ universities.


On the side, I'm also writing a 60+ page honors thesis on fandom and community behaviors, volunteering at my local food pantry, and growing my niche earring collection.
